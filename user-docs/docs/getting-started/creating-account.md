# Creating Your Account

Learn how to create your Communities account and set up your profile.

## Prerequisites

Before creating an account, you'll need:

- An email address
- An invitation link or code from an existing community (optional, depending on configuration)

## Step-by-Step Account Creation

### 1. Navigate to the Registration Page

Visit the Communities instance URL and click **Sign Up** or **Create Account**.

### 2. Fill in Your Details

Provide the following information:

- **Email**: Your email address (used for login and notifications)
- **Username**: Choose a unique username (visible to community members)
- **Password**: Create a secure password
  - Minimum 8 characters
  - Mix of letters, numbers, and symbols recommended
- **Display Name**: How you want to appear to others (can be changed later)

### 3. Verify Your Email

After registration:

1. Check your email inbox
2. Click the verification link
3. Your account is now active

!!! tip "Email Not Received?"
    Check your spam folder. If still missing, click "Resend verification email" on the login page.

## Setting Up Your Profile

Once logged in, complete your profile:

### 1. Access Profile Settings

Click your avatar â†’ **Profile Settings**

### 2. Add Profile Information

- **Display Name**: How others see you
- **Bio**: Brief description about yourself
- **Avatar**: Upload a profile picture (optional)
- **Location**: City/region (optional, helps local communities)
- **Skills**: List your skills (used for skill endorsements)

### 3. Configure Privacy Settings

Choose what information is visible:

- **Profile visibility**: Public, members-only, or private
- **Contact information**: Who can see your email
- **Activity visibility**: Show your community participation

## Joining Your First Community

After account creation:

### With an Invitation Link

1. Click the invitation link you received
2. Accept the invitation
3. You're now a community member!

### Without an Invitation

1. Browse public communities (if enabled)
2. Request to join a community
3. Wait for admin approval

!!! note "Community Access"
    Some communities require invitations for privacy. Ask a member for an invite link.

## Understanding Your Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:

- **My Communities**: Communities you're a member of
- **Trust Score**: Your trust level in each community
- **Recent Activity**: Latest updates and notifications
- **My Requests**: Resource requests you've made
- **My Shares**: Resources you're sharing

## Account Security

### Best Practices

- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication (if available)
- Don't share your password
- Log out on shared computers

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to **Profile Settings**
2. Click **Security**
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password twice
5. Click **Update Password**

### Forgot Password?

1. Click **Forgot Password** on login page
2. Enter your email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create a new password

## Deleting Your Account

To delete your account:

1. Go to **Profile Settings**
2. Click **Delete Account** (bottom of page)
3. Confirm deletion
4. Your data will be removed within 30 days

!!! warning "Account Deletion"
    This action is permanent. Your trust awards, contributions, and shared resources will be removed.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

- Verify email address is correct
- Check if email is verified
- Try password reset
- Clear browser cache

### Email Verification Issues

- Check spam/junk folder
- Request new verification email
- Contact support if persistent

### Username Already Taken

- Try adding numbers or variations
- Username must be unique across the platform
